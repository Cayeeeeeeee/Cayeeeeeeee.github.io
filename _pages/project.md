Photoelectrocatalysis for CO2 Reduction
======

I am interested in interfacial design strategies for photoelectrocatalysts that enable selective CO2 reduction. This work focuses on regulating surface active sites, charge transfer, and reaction pathways at the photoelectrode/electrolyte interface.

Glycerol Upgrading by Cascade Catalysis
======

My research also explores cascade photoelectrocatalytic oxidation of glycerol toward value-added chemicals. A recent direction combines photoelectrocatalysis with biocatalysis to achieve selective molecular upgrading.

Photoelectrocatalytic Device Engineering
======

I work on photoelectrocatalytic device design for green organic synthesis, with an emphasis on integrating catalyst interfaces, light absorption, charge transport, and reaction environments.
