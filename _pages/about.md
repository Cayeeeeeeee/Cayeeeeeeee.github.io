I am Caiyi Liu, a Ph.D. student in Nano Science and Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Dong Liu and Prof. Yujie Xiong. My research focuses on photoelectrocatalysis for selective carbon dioxide reduction, biomass-derived molecule upgrading, and device engineering for green organic synthesis.

Research Interests
======

* Interfacial design of photoelectrocatalysts for selective CO2 reduction
* Cascade photoelectrocatalytic oxidation of glycerol to value-added chemicals
* Photoelectrocatalytic device engineering for green organic synthesis

Education

  * Advisors: Prof. Dong Liu and Prof. Yujie Xiong
* 2018-2022: B.S., New Energy Science and Engineering, Southwest Petroleum University
