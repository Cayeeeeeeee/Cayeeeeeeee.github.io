I am a Ph.D. student in Nano Science and Engineering at USTC, advised by Prof. Dong Liu and Prof. Yujie Xiong. My research focuses on photoelectrocatalysis for CO2 reduction, glycerol upgrading, and green organic synthesis.

Email: [lcy_id@mail.ustc.edu.cn](mailto:lcy_id@mail.ustc.edu.cn)

Research Interests
------

* Photoelectrocatalyst interface design for selective CO2 reduction
* Cascade photoelectrocatalytic oxidation of glycerol to value-added chemicals
* Photoelectrocatalytic device engineering for green organic synthesis
